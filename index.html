ABOUT ME

I've been working on interfaces since 2001 and on UX even before the term was popularized. As a designer, my main goal is always to think about the user/consumer needs.

My background is in Graphic Design, but after graduation I also studied Product Design, having proficiency on both.

I was responsible for the development of physical and digital products that are used by big companies such as Itau, Banco do Brasil, Totvs, Senior Sistemas and other national and international companies. Their datacenters have some hardware designed by me, as so is their DCIM (Datacenter Infrastructure Management System) in which I was responsible for UX and interface. We won an award with this project in 2012, named “Ideas for the Future, Innovation and Design”.

I also have experience in UX for video games.
